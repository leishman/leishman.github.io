I’ve been quite negligent of this blog for a few weeks now. I feel somewhat guilty about it, but I think it was for the better. I needed some time to reflect, think and be productive. This is what has happened since my last post:

1) I went to India. 
I took a short 5 day trip to Delhi and Agra. India is a very interesting country and I would definitely like to go back at some point with friends and more of a solid plan.


2) I spent 2 weeks in Bangkok working on my programming and Thai skills. 

Moving from place to place was wearing me out, so I decided to plant my roots and make my home in Bangkok for a few weeks. I had a great time and made some good friends at my hostel in the Silom area. I feel like a Bangkok local now and feel at home in the city.

My Dev Bootcamp prep work has started, so that kept me busy. I also spent some time working through a book on Metaprogramming in Ruby. 

Trying to make the most of my time, I took 5 private Thai lessons and started catching on pretty quickly. I can count and verbalize some basic thoughts in Thai now, although I’m worried that if I don’t practice when I come home I’ll forget a lot of my vocabulary. I guess I’ll have to eat at a lot of Thai restaurants.

I also had some fun nights in the city, but I think it’s best if those details remain in my head.


3) My parents arrived and I’m spending the next 1.5 weeks showing them around Thailand and Cambodia.

I’ll put those updates in a new post.
