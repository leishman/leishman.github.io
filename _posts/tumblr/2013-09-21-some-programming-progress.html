I’ve made some progress with Maply, although I haven’t been as productive as I would like. Tokyo has been distracting me from work a bit, but I’m hoping to put in a good full day or two when I get to Bangkok.

Next, I have to find a good dataset and figure out the most effective way of displaying the information in a simple and intuitive fashion.

I’ve created a kids version of Maply with bare-bones information. The normal version of Maply will have more in depth data and interactive content.
