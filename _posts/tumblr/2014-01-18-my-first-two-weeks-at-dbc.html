It has been a LONG while since my last blog post. I made it back from my travels and had a great Christmas season with my family.

On New Year’s Eve I moved to San Francisco and shortly thereafter started at Dev Bootcamp.

I’ve been in the program for two weeks now and it has been a great experience so far. The structured coursework and experienced instructors have been very effective in solidifying my programming skills. However, I find the most valuable part of DBC is the atmosphere of self-education at the school.

I have been staying until at least 11pm every night because I thoroughly enjoy working in this space and diving deep into concepts I find interesting and important.Â 

The current phase of the course is focused on building up our programming skills in Ruby. This part of the course has been somewhat slow for me because I already have an engineering background, but I have had no trouble finding “stretch” exercises to do and extra side projects to work on. This extra work is what I find the most valuable anyways.

I’ve realized that one of the best ways to solidify concepts in my head is to teach somebody else about it, so I will be using this blog as a place to describe interesting programming/tech concepts that I come across.
