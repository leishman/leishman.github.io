However, I can’t say I have a clear mind. I’ve got a lot of mixed feelings and I need to spend some time figuring everything out. Although I’ve made some programming progress, it has not been as much as I had hoped for. Balancing my desire to explore the places I visit with my desire to improve my craft hasn’t been easy.
