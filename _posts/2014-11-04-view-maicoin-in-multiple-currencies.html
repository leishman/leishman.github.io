mai-coin:

We want MaiCoin to be easy to use for people all over the world. That’s why you can now view the site in multiple currencies! To change the currency displayed on maicoin.com, simply choose from the new drop-down menu on the navbar.

After changing the currency, the buy/sell bitcoin section will still be displayed in TWD because this is the only currency we currently support for placing orders.

We are working hard to support multiple currencies for buy and sell orders. Stay tuned!
